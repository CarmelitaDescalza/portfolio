What I Do

I like to work on problem solving. Understanding the problem, and finding a creative way to solve it, with a design that works and if it can be exciting, even better.

I enjoy learning, I like to explore ways to access knowledge through logic and aesthetics ways.

I am enthusiastic about knowing ways of expressing, emotions, messages, knowledge, experiences, whatever, and learning how to shape it, to give it form through a text, a song, a drawing, a scene, a design, and who knows what we will be able to express with the new technological tools

That's why I studied Industrial Design, History and Performing Arts and after all this I studied the Higher National Diploma in Multiplatform Application Development.

I try to be always aware of trends and especially of new technologies. I am interested in technology because I am captivated by its enormous potential and projection in infinite fields. Each new programming language, development environment, each topic that I advance in this learning, is like acquiring a new brush or color to start painting in this almost blank canvas of the technological universe.

I feel that we are in prehistory, at the beginning of this evolution, I want to be part of this new era in an active way, among other things, because I would love to participate in projects that know how to use this enormous power to try to make the world a better place.

I learned the following languages and frameworks: SQL, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, XML, Swift, HTML5, CSS, PHP, C#, Spring, Angular 8, AndroidStudio, XCode, among others.

I am still studying especially frontend, product design, design and tools to generate immersive experiences. I am aware that you learn to work by working. That's why I engage, whenever I can, in collaborations to work on real projects and with professional teams.

The future of my career, will be marked by the people who demand my presence in their professional work team.

I am eager that someone trusts me to make that happen and start the new journey.

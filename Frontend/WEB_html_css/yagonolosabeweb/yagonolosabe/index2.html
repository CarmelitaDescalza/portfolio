What I Do



El verdadero éxito sería comunicar todo el texto que viene a continuación con simples dibujos y breves palabras. Estoy trabajando en ello. Disculpen.


I'm a Product Designer , UX/UI apprentice and Junior Frontend Developer.

I am passionate about design, art and performing arts.

I believe in people and in the possibility we all have to give the best version of ourselves. I believe that we should try to make this world a little bit better.

I enjoy learning, I like to explore ways to access knowledge through logic and aesthetics ways.

I like to work on problem solving. Understanding the problem, the message, the channel, the receiver and finding a creative way to solve it, with a design that works and if it can be exciting, even better.

I am enthusiastic about knowing ways of expressing, emotions, messages, knowledge, experiences, whatever, and learning how to shape it, to give it form through a text, a song, a drawing, a scene, a design,... who knows what we will be able to express with the new technological tools

That's why I studied Industrial Design, History and Performing Arts.

I try to be always aware of trends and especially of new technologies, that's why I studied the Higher National Diploma in Multiplatform Application Development.

I am interested in technology because I am captivated by its enormous potential and projection in infinite fields. Each new programming language, development environment, each topic that I advance in this learning, is like acquiring a new brush or color to start painting in this almost blank canvas of the technological universe.

As Vannevar Bush predicted in 1945, I believe that technology increases man's physical abilities and, above all, his intellectual capacity. I feel that we are in prehistory, at the beginning of this evolution, I want to be part of this new era in an active way, among other things, because I would love to participate in projects that know how to use this enormous power to try to make the world a better place.

In school we have acquired the tools to become fullstack developers.

I learned the following languages and frameworks: SQL, JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, XML, Swift, HTML5, CSS, PHP, C#, Spring, Angular 8, AndroidStudio, XCode, among others.

I am prepared and willing to start my professional career in the fields of develop backend, frontend, Internet of Things, video games and RA, and/or, product design, design UX/UI.

I am still studying especially frontend and product design pero I am aware that you learn to work by working. The future of my career, the evolution and specialization of my learning in some of the fields in which I have started, will be marked by the people who demand my presence in their professional work team.

I am eager that someone trusts me to make that happen and start the new journey.
